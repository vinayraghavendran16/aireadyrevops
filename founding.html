Questions before you apply

Why would you give away a $12,500 audit?

Because right now a founding case study is worth more to me than the fee. The framework is published; what I need is undeniable proof it works on real companies. Three founding clients get the full engagement free; I get three case studies. After that, the price is $12,500 — this isn't a recurring discount.

What's the actual catch?

Three things, none of them money: you take the 4-minute assessment, you introduce three peers who should know their own score, and — if selected — you let me write up the engagement as a case study (named or anonymized, your call).

Do my three referrals have to buy anything?

No. They get the same free 4-minute assessment, no obligation. The introductions just show me you're serious and help me reach the leaders this is built for.

How much of my time does the audit take?

Minimal. It's async-first: read-only system access, a couple of short interviews in your timezone, and a 60-minute readout. You don't build anything — I do the analysis.

What if I'm not selected?

You keep your assessment score and personalized report regardless, and you'll have first access to the paid audit. No spot lost, nothing wasted.

Is this really limited to three?

Yes. I can only deliver a small number of audits at this depth at once, and the offer exists to generate three case studies — not a pipeline of free work. When the third is locked, it closes.
