Buyer FAQs

Is there a guarantee?

Yes — the Triple-Lock Guarantee. The Verdict must identify at least 3× its fee ($37,500) in recoverable waste plus cost of inaction, or you don't pay. Your fee credits 100% toward a Recovery Project booked within 60 days. And if you sit through the readout and don't think it was worth it, you get a full refund and keep the report. The Verdict carries the risk, not you.

What's the difference between this and a generic RevOps audit?

A generic audit tells you your stack is messy. The Verdict answers the question your board is actually asking: what did the AI spend return, where is it leaking, and what recovers it. Same depth — six weighted dimensions, 24 published criteria, evidence-backed — but it ends with a board-grade number and the two fixes that recover the most, not a deck that gets shelved.

Do I need the paid Verdict, or is the free assessment enough?

The free assessment is directional — useful for executives who want to know roughly where they stand. The Verdict produces evidence-backed scoring with system access, a defensible board-grade number, the leakage lines, and the two highest-recovery fixes. If you're not sure your team even agrees on definitions yet, start with the $3,500 Definition Lock — it credits fully toward the Verdict. Most teams scoring 35–79 on the self-assessment benefit from the Verdict.

Which CRM platforms do you support?

Salesforce and HubSpot natively, with deep architectural experience in both. Other platforms (Zoho, Microsoft Dynamics, Pipedrive) on a case-by-case basis. The framework is platform-agnostic; the audit methodology adapts to your stack. Multi-platform stacks (e.g., Salesforce + HubSpot + Marketo) are explicitly in scope.

How does delivery work for clients outside India?

Async-first by design. All deliverables are written, with stakeholder interviews scheduled in your timezone (PT, ET, UK, AU all comfortable). Tier 1 requires only 2 calls. Tier 2 requires 6 interviews, all in your business hours. The executive readout is recorded as a Loom for stakeholders who couldn't attend live.

What system access do you need?

Read-only access to your CRM (Salesforce or HubSpot), marketing automation (Marketo, HubSpot, Pardot), and any critical revenue tool. We never write to production. Access can be time-boxed (we recommend 4 weeks). NDA executed before any access is granted.

What happens after the Verdict?

The Verdict produces a 90-day recovery roadmap with effort and impact per item. From there, clients typically take one of three paths: a Recovery Project ($40K–$80K, 8–12 weeks) to fix the leaks, a Quarterly AI Revenue Review ($4K–$6K/quarter) to keep the number honest for the board, or a fractional RevOps Architect retainer ($8K–$12K/month). All are optional. The Verdict stands alone as a deliverable.

How quickly does the Verdict pay back?

Most Verdict clients recover $50K–$150K in the first 60 days through identified cost savings, license optimization, and recovered time. Median payback period is 45–90 days. The leakage lines are quantified, so your CFO can see the financial impact and your board can see the number.

Productization. The methodology is published, templates exist, and there's no army of associates to bill out. You get the same depth — 24 scoring criteria across 6 dimensions, evidence-backed — without the consulting markup. If your recovery work is large, that's where bigger numbers live; the Verdict is the wedge, not the destination.
